Developed MoUGAN combining MobileNetV3 and UNet for lightweight multi-scale image restoration. Achieved 36.36 dB PSNR, 0.9211 SSIM, and 18.79s inference on GoPro/Köhler datasets. Modules: dataset augmentation, self-attention, bidirectional feature fusion, cascaded dilated convolutions. Full PyTorch pipeline with degradation modeling, GAN loss, and perceptual loss. 09/2023–06/2024.
